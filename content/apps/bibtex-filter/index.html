BibTeX Filter

This web app helps in filtering bibtex entries from a large bibtex database. Usecase: Suppose you start drafting a paper with a large BibTeX db with thousands of entries(e.g., ACL Anothology.bib, or all your previous references); later if you decide to reduce size of your referece db by selecting only the subset of articles that you actually used in your paper.
